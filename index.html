Welcome to Priou.

Hi! You're probably wondering what Priou does. Well, Priou builds software. Based in London, we have 15 years of experience building and maintaining systems in a variety of industries (with an emphasis on finance). We've been involved since early on in .Net. We love the web and always have. We're passionate about open source; we build it and we use it. If you need a system built, we may be able to help you!
